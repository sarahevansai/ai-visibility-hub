## 📰 Press Release Research

### 17. Press release citations account for just 0.04% of AI citations (syndicated wire)
- **Source:** ALM Corp analysis of BuzzStream/XOFU study (Mar 2026)
- **URL:** https://almcorp.com/blog/ai-search-press-release-citations/
- **Methodology:** BuzzStream used XOFU (Citation Labs) to track 4M+ AI citation sources across ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Google AI Mode, Google Gemini. 3,600 prompts × 10 industries over 1 week.
- **Detail:**
  - Syndicated PRs via Yahoo Finance / MSN: 0.04% of all citations
  - Direct wire service citations (PRNewswire): 0.21%

- **Caveat:** BuzzStream sells outreach tools — directional findings consistent with independent research.
- **Used in:** Press release key stat (negative: wire ≠ AI)
